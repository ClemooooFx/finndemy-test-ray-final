Finndemy User Guide - Learners

Account Registration & Login

How to Create Your Finndemy Account
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click the "Sign Up" button at the top right

Fill in the Registration Form:

  • Username: Must be unique (cannot be changed later)
  • Email: Enter a valid email for verification
  • Password: Minimum 8 characters, secure format recommended
  • Full Name: First and last name used on certificates
  • Privacy Policy: Check the agreement box
  • Click "Complete Sign Up" to submit

Email Verification:

  • Check your inbox (or spam folder) for a verification email
  • Click the activation link in the message
  • After activation, you can now log in using your credentials
How to Log in
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click the "Log In" button on the top right
  • Enter your registered username and password
  • Check the "Remember Me" box if you're using a trusted device
  • Click Login
  • You are now successfully logged into your Finndemy account
Forgot Password? How to Reset
  • Visit finndemy.com and click “Log In”
  • Click on “Lost your Password” if your login fails
  • Enter the email you registered with and click “Reset Password”
  • A confirmation message will appear; check your email inbox
  • Open the email and click the “Click Here” link
  • You will be redirected to a reset page
  • Enter a new strong password and click Save
  • If prompted by your browser, you may save the password for future logins
  • You’ll see a confirmation that your password was reset successfully
How to Log Out
  • While logged in, go to the top right corner of the page
  • Below the support email, your username will be displayed with a “Logout” icon
  • Click “Log Out”
  • You will be logged out and redirected to the Finndemy homepage
How to Enroll in a Course

Browse Courses

  • Visit the “Explore Courses” section from the main menu
  • Use the search bar to type the course name (optional)
  • Click “Enroll Now” on a course that interests you

Course Information

  • Review course description and key topics

Enrollment Process

  • Click “Enroll in this course”
  • Review course details and pricing
  • Click “Add to cart”
  • A pop-up will confirm it's added; click “View Cart”
  • Review cart, apply coupon if available, then click “Proceed to Checkout”
  • Enter billing details, choose payment method
  • Agree to terms and click “Place Order”
  • Complete secure checkout
  • If using Mpesa, enter PIN when prompted
  • If prompt fails, click “Resend Payment Prompt”
  • After successful payment, click “Proceed”
  • Receive confirmation email
  • Access course materials immediately
Your Learning Dashboard

Track your progress, access certificates, and interact with the learning community from your dashboard.

How to View and Edit Your User Profile
  • After logging in, click your profile icon on the top right
  • You’ll see a summary of your progress
  • Click “Edit Profile” to update your details
  • Change your name, profile photo, or cover image
  • You can also update your password from this section
