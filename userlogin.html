Finndemy User Guide - Learners

Account Registration & Login

How to Create Your Finndemy Account

Step 1: Access the Registration Page

  • Navigate to finndemy.com
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right

Step 2: Fill in Your Information

  • Username: Choose a unique username (cannot be changed later)
  • Email Address: Use a valid email for account verification
  • Password: Create a strong password (min 8 characters)
  • First Name and Last Name: Enter your real name for certificates
  • Accept the privacy policy: Tick the checkbox
  • Click “Complete Sign Up”

Step 3: Verify Your Email

  • Check your email inbox or spam folder for a verification message
  • Click the verification link in the email
  • Click “Activate”

✅ Your account was activated successfully! You can now log in using the username and password you provided.

How to Log in
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click the "Log In" button on the top right
  • Enter your registered username and password
  • Check the "Remember Me" box if you're using a trusted device
  • Click Login
  • You are now successfully logged into your Finndemy account
Forgot Password? How to Reset
  • Visit finndemy.com and click “Log In”
  • Click on “Lost your Password” if your login fails
  • Enter the email you registered with and click “Reset Password”
  • A confirmation message will appear; check your email inbox
  • Open the email and click the “Click Here” link
  • You will be redirected to a reset page
  • Enter a new strong password and click Save
  • If prompted by your browser, you may save the password for future logins
  • You’ll see a confirmation that your password was reset successfully
How to Log Out
  • While logged in, go to the top right corner of the page
  • Below the support email, your username will be displayed with a “Logout” icon
  • Click “Log Out”
  • You will be logged out and redirected to the Finndemy homepage
How to Enroll in a Course

Browse Courses

  • Visit the "Explore Courses" section from the main menu
  • Use the search bar and type the name of the course (optional)
  • From the available courses, choose any that piques your interest by clicking “Enroll Now”

Course Information

  • Course Description: Overview of what you'll learn
  • Key Topics: Key areas that will be covered in the course

Enrollment Process

  • Click “Enroll in this course”
  • Review course details and pricing
  • Click “Add to cart”
  • A pop-up will show course added to cart, click “View cart”
  • Review cart content, apply coupon if available
  • Click “Proceed to Checkout”
  • Enter billing details and select payment method
  • Agree to the terms and click “Place Order”
  • Choose your preferred payment method and complete the secure checkout
  • If using Mpesa, a prompt will appear — enter your PIN and tap “Send”
  • If prompt doesn't show, click “Resend Payment Prompt” or follow additional instructions
  • After successful payment, click Proceed
  • Receive enrollment confirmation email
  • Access course materials immediately after payment
Your Learning Dashboard

Track your progress, access certificates, and interact with the learning community from your dashboard.

How to View and Edit Your User Profile
  • After logging in, click your profile icon on the top right
  • You’ll see a summary of your progress
  • Click “Edit Profile” to update your details
  • Change your name, profile photo, or cover image
  • You can also update your password from this section
How to Give Feedback on a Course
  • Click on any course to open its page
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and find the “Leave a Reply” section
  • Type your comment about the course
  • Click “Post Comment”
  • Your comment will be previewed and held for moderation
  • Once approved, it will become publicly visible
  • Alternatively, enrolled users can go to the “Reviews” tab on the course page
  • You must be enrolled and have started the course to leave a review
