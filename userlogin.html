Account Registration & Login

 
  How to Create Your Finndemy Account  

Step 1: Access the Registration Page

 
       
  • Navigate to finndemy.com
    •    
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right
    •  
   

Step 2: Fill in Your Information

 
       
  • Username: Choose a unique username (cannot be changed later)
    •    
  • Email Address: Use a valid email for account verification
    •    
  • Password: Create a strong password (min 8 characters)
    •    
  • First Name and Last Name: Enter your real name for certificates
    •    
  • Accept the privacy policy: Tick the checkbox
    •    
  • Click “Complete Sign Up”
    •  
     

Step 3: Verify Your Email

 
       
  • Check your email inbox or spam folder for a verification message
    •  
   
       
  • Click the verification link in the email
    •  
     
       
  • Click “Activate”
    •  
   

✅ Your account was activated successfully! You can now log in using the username and password you provided.

---  
  How to Log in  
       
  • Go to finndemy.com
    •    
  • On the top right corner, click “Log In”
    •  
   
       
  • Enter your registered username and password
    •    
  • Tick the “Remember Me” box if using a trusted device for future auto-login
    •  
   
       
  • Click “Login”
    •    
  • You have now successfully logged in to your Finndemy account
    •

  Forgot Password? How to Reset  
       
  • Go to finndemy.com
    •    
  • Click “Log In” in the top right area
    •  
   
       
  • Enter your email address and password and click Login
    •  
   
       
  • If your password is incorrect, click on “Lost your Password”
    •  
   
       
  • On the next page, enter the email you used to sign up and click “Reset Password”
    •  
   
       
  • You will see a confirmation that the password reset email has been sent. Check your email.
    •  
   
       
  • Check among your most recent emails.
    •  
   
       
  • Open the password reset email and click the “Click Here” link
    •  
   
       
  • You will be redirected to a page asking you to reset your password
    •  
   
       
  • Enter a new, strong password you will remember and click Save
    •  
   
       
  • Your browser may prompt you to save your password. If you're on a trusted device, you may click “Save”
    •  
   
       
  • You’ll see a confirmation that your password has been successfully reset. You can now log in with your updated credentials.
    •  
 
---  
  How to Log Out  
       
  • Once you are logged in, go to the top right corner of the page.
    •    
  • Below the support email, your username will appear with a “Logout” icon.
    •  
   
       
  • Click “Log Out”. You will be signed out and redirected to the Finndemy homepage (finndemy.com).
    •

  How to Enroll in a Course  

Browse Courses

 
       
  • Visit the "Explore Courses" section from the main menu
    •  
   
       
  • Use the search bar and type the name of the course (optional)
    •  
   
       
  • From the available courses, choose any that piques your interest by clicking “Enroll Now”
    •  
   

Course Information

 
       
  • Course Description: Overview of what you'll learn
    •    
  • Key Topics: Key areas that will be covered in the course
    •  
   

Enrollment Process

 
       
  • Click “Enroll in this course”
    •  
   
       
  • Review course details and pricing
    •    
  • Click “Add to cart”
    •  
   
       
  • A pop-up will show course added to cart, click “View cart”
    •  
   
       
  • Review cart content, apply coupon if available
    •    
  • Click “Proceed to Checkout”
    •  
   
       
  • Enter billing details and select payment method
    •  
   
       
  • Agree to the terms and click “Place Order”
    •  
   
       
  • Choose your preferred payment method and complete the secure checkout
    •  
   
       
  • If using Mpesa, a prompt will appear — enter your PIN and tap “Send”
    •  
   
       
  • If prompt doesn't show, click “Resend Payment Prompt” or follow additional instructions
    •  
   
       
  • After successful payment, click Proceed
    •  
   
       
  • Receive enrollment confirmation email
    •    
  • Access course materials immediately after payment
    •

  How to Give Feedback on a Course  
       
  • After clicking on any course, scroll to the bottom of the course page to find the “Leave a Reply” section. From here, you can leave a comment about the course.
    •  
   
       
  • Once you're done typing, click on “Post Comment”
    •  
   
       
  • Your comment will now be awaiting moderation. A preview is shown immediately; the comment becomes visible once approved.
    •  
   
       
  • Alternatively, if you have enrolled in the course, go to the “Reviews” tab to give structured feedback.
    •  
   
       
  • 🔒 Note: You must be enrolled and have started the course to be eligible to submit a review.
