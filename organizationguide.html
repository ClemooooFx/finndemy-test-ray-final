Managing Access and Reporting

How to Register your Organization on Finndemy

Step 1: Sign Up

  • Visit https://finndemy.com
  • Click on the Sign Up button.
  • Register using your email address or Google account.

🔐 Step 2: Log In

  • After registering, log in with your credentials.
  • You will be redirected to your account dashboard.

📌 Step 3: Access Business Plans

  • In the top menu, click FINNDEMY PLANS.

✅ Step 4: Fill Out the Business Registration Form

Once you've clicked FINNDEMY PLANS, you will land on the Business Registration form page (as shown in the image).

  1. First Name – Your first name.
  2. Last Name – Your surname.
  3. Enter your work email address – Must be the email you used to sign up on Finndemy.
  4. Your phone number – Provide a valid phone number.
  5. Organization name – Enter your company/business name.
  6. Number of seats (Employees) – Indicate how many employee accounts you'll need.

📤 Step 5: Submit

  • Click the “Register Business” button.
  • Your registration will be submitted for review.

✅ Step 6: Registration Confirmation

Once you submit the form, you'll see a confirmation message like this:

  • Your submitted details (Business Name, Contact Info, Seats, etc.) will be displayed.
  • 📩 Status Message:
  • ⏳ “Your group is being processed. We'll email you when ready.”

This means the Finndemy team is reviewing your registration. You’ll receive an email once your group account is set up and active.

How to Buy Courses for Your Organization with Finndemy Plans

✅ Step 1: Go to the Home Page

  • While logged into your organizational account, click on “Home” in the top navigation bar.
  • 🔍 This will show all available courses, including the ones available to your group.
  • 📌 Make sure you're logged in with the email linked to your organization’s registration to see group-access features.

✅ Step 2: Scroll Down and Click “Finndemy Plans”

  • Scroll to the bottom of the Home page and click on the “Finndemy Plans” link in the green footer section.
  • 🔗 This will take you to your organization’s available course bundle or plan.

🛒 Click the “Buy Now” button under your preferred plan to proceed with purchase.

✅ Step 4: Choose the Package Duration and Add to Cart

  1. Select the Package:
    • Click on the dropdown labeled "Package".
    • Choose between:
      • Bi-Annual (KES 1,950,000)
      • Annual (KES 2,550,000)
    • In your screenshot, "Annual" is selected.
  2. Review the Group Name and Seats:
    • Example Group Name: Business Enterprise | 06/04/2025
    • Seats: 1 (You can update this based on the number of users needed.)
  3. Click “Add to cart”. Once added, proceed to checkout to finalize your purchase.

Step 5: Proceed to Checkout

Now, click the “View cart” button (highlighted in your screenshot) to:

  1. Review your cart contents – Confirm that the correct package (Annual) and quantity (number of seats) are selected.

Click on “Proceed to checkout” – This will take you to the payment and billing section.

Step 6: Finalize Your Purchase

  • Click on “Proceed to checkout” to:
    • Enter your billing details
    • Choose a payment method
