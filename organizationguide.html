Managing Access and Reporting

How to Register your Organization on Finndemy

Step 1: Sign Up

  • Visit https://finndemy.com
  • Click on the Sign Up button.
  • Register using your email address or Google account.

🔐 Step 2: Log In

  • After registering, log in with your credentials.
  • You will be redirected to your account dashboard.

📌 Step 3: Access Business Plans

  • In the top menu, click FINNDEMY PLANS.

✅ Step 4: Fill Out the Business Registration Form

Once you've clicked FINNDEMY PLANS, you will land on the Business Registration form page (as shown in the image).

  1. First Name – Your first name.
  2. Last Name – Your surname.
  3. Enter your work email address – Must be the email you used to sign up on Finndemy.
  4. Your phone number – Provide a valid phone number.
  5. Organization name – Enter your company/business name.
  6. Number of seats (Employees) – Indicate how many employee accounts you'll need.

📤 Step 5: Submit

  • Click the “Register Business” button.
  • Your registration will be submitted for review.

✅ Step 6: Registration Confirmation

Once you submit the form, you'll see a confirmation message like this:

  • Your submitted details (Business Name, Contact Info, Seats, etc.) will be displayed.
  • 📩 Status Message:
  • ⏳ “Your group is being processed. We'll email you when ready.”

This means the Finndemy team is reviewing your registration. You’ll receive an email once your group account is set up and active.
