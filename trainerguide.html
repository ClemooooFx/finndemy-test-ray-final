Finndemy User Guide - Trainers

Course Setup & Certification

Enabling Certificates and CPD Hours
  • Turn on certificate generation for your courses.
  • Set minimum completion requirements for students to earn certificates.
  • Specify CPD hours which reflect course duration and learning activities.
  • Courses can be aligned with industry and professional body accreditations.
How to Create a Course
  • Log in and go to the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Click on "Create New Course".
  • Enter your course title and description.
  • Choose and upload a featured image:
    • Select the image and click “Open”.
    • After upload, click “Select” at the bottom right.
  • Click “Save to Draft” at the top right to save your progress.

Continue to: How to Add Course Content

How to Add Course Content
  • Go to the Course Builder.
  • Click "Add New Lesson".
  • Set the lesson title, featured image, and upload content (text, PDFs, videos, audio, etc.).
  • Configure Advanced Options:
    • Video Progression, Assignment Uploads, Lesson Timer.
    • Use tags, categories, and lesson URL for structure.
  • Add optional materials under the Material tab.
  • Set access options (sample lesson, release schedule).
  • Use drag & drop to organize lessons. Use pencil icon to edit, X to delete, checkmark to publish.
How to Adjust Course Settings
  • Access Settings: Open, Buy Now, Recurring, or Closed (with manual/group enrollment).
  • Navigation: Linear (sequential) or Free Form (non-sequential).
  • Display: Toggle visibility, add course material, certificates, challenge exam if needed.
  • Course Users: Assign/remove users manually. Group-managed users appear in Group Admin.
  • Co-Instructors: Add instructors to co-manage content.
  • Extend Access: Extend course access for enrolled students via date picker.
  • Manage Groups: Assign or unassign user groups via group interface.
How to Publish a Course
  • Ensure Course Page, Builder, Settings, and Access/Groups are completed.
  • Click the blue “Publish” button at the top-right.
  • Once published, the course becomes visible unless marked private or hidden.
  • You can always revert to draft if needed.

Earnings & Payment Details

Commission Structure & Payment
  • Trainers earn 20% commission of course sales revenue.
  • Payments are processed monthly by the 15th, provided minimum threshold is met.
  • Payment methods include M-PESA, PayPal, bank transfers, and more.
  • Track your earnings and payments with real-time dashboard analytics.

Account Management

How to Set Up Your Trainer Account
  • Access Profile → upload headshot, go to Instructor Dashboard → Settings.
  • Fill in biography, education, achievements, links, and portfolio.
  • Add PayPal email and set up notifications.
  • Submit documents for verification and complete onboarding steps.
How to Register as a Trainer
  • Go to finndemy.com and click "Become an Instructor".
  • Fill in your details: name, email, phone, country, username, password.
  • Agree to privacy policy and submit the form.
  • Check your inbox for a confirmation email.
How to Reset Your Password
  • Click “Log in” → “Lost your Password”.
  • Enter your email and click “Reset Password”.
  • Check your inbox for a reset link → enter a new strong password and save.
  • Log in using your new credentials.
How to Log Out
  • Click your username at the top right, then click the “Logout” icon.
  • You’ll be redirected to the homepage.

Course Deletion & Restoration

How to Delete a Course

Method 1: From Course Editor

  • Open the course editor → Click “Move to Trash” in the top-left.

Method 2: From Course List

  • Go to Dashboard → Course List → Click trash icon under the course.

To Permanently Delete:

  • Go to the Trash tab → click trash icon again → confirm deletion.
  • Warning: This is irreversible.
How to Restore a Deleted Course
  • Go to Course List → Trash tab.
  • Click the Restore icon under the course you wish to recover.
  • The course will return to its previous state (draft or published).
