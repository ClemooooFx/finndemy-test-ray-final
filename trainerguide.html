Course Setup & Certification

Enabling Certificates and CPD Hours
  • Turn on certificate generation for your courses.
  • Set minimum completion requirements for students to earn certificates.
  • Specify CPD hours which reflect course duration and learning activities.
  • Courses can be aligned with industry and professional body accreditations.
How to Create a Course
  • Log in and go to the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Click on "Create New Course".
  • Enter your course title and description.
  • Choose and upload a featured image:
    • Select the image and click “Open”.
    • After upload, click “Select” at the bottom right.
  • Click “Save to Draft” at the top right to save your progress.

Continue to: How to Add Course Content

How to Add Course Content
  • Go to the Course Builder.
  • Click "Add New Lesson".
  • Set the lesson title, featured image, and upload content (text, PDFs, videos, audio, etc.).
  • Configure Advanced Options:
    • Video Progression, Assignment Uploads, Lesson Timer.
    • Use tags, categories, and lesson URL for structure.
  • Add optional materials under the Material tab.
  • Set access options (sample lesson, release schedule).
  • Use drag & drop to organize lessons. Use pencil icon to edit, X to delete, checkmark to publish.
How to Adjust Course Settings
  • Access Settings: Open, Buy Now, Recurring, or Closed (with manual/group enrollment).
  • Navigation: Linear (sequential) or Free Form (non-sequential).
  • Display: Toggle visibility, add course material, certificates, challenge exam if needed.
  • Course Users: Assign/remove users manually. Group-managed users appear in Group Admin.
  • Co-Instructors: Add instructors to co-manage content.
  • Extend Access: Extend course access for enrolled students via date picker.
  • Manage Groups: Assign or unassign user groups via group interface.
How to Publish a Course
  • Ensure Course Page, Builder, Settings, and Access/Groups are completed.
  • Click the blue “Publish” button at the top-right.
  • Once published, the course becomes visible unless marked private or hidden.
  • You can always revert to draft if needed.

Earnings & Payment Details

Commission Structure & Payment
  • Trainers earn 20% commission of course sales revenue.
  • Payments are processed monthly by the 15th, provided minimum threshold is met.
  • Payment methods include M-PESA, PayPal, bank transfers, and more.
  • Track your earnings and payments with real-time dashboard analytics.

Account Management

How to Set Up Your Trainer Account
  1. Access Your Profile
    • Log in to your account
    • Click on “Profile Details” to upload a professional headshot (minimum 300x300px)
    • Navigate to "Instructor Dashboard"
    • On the navigation menu, the last item, click on “Settings”
  2. Complete Your Trainer Profile
    • You can change your basic details here
    • Bio Section: Write a compelling 200–300 word biography
    • Add Education history
    • Add Achievements: Include any relevant achievements
    • Social Media Links: Add professional social profiles
    • Website/Portfolio: Link to your professional website
  3. Banking and Payment Setup
    • Navigate to "PayPal"
    • Write your PayPal email
  4. Notification Preferences
    • Email notifications for new enrollments
    • Student message and quiz completion alerts
  5. Account Verification and Approval
    1. Document Submission
      • Identity verification (government-issued ID)
      • Educational certificates
      • Professional credentials
      • Portfolio or work samples
    2. Background Check Process
      • Review typically takes 3–5 business days
      • You'll receive email updates on status
      • Additional information may be requested
    3. Platform Orientation
      • Complete mandatory orientation modules
      • Attend live onboarding session (scheduled upon approval)
How to Register as a Trainer

Account Creation and Setup

  1. Access the Registration Page
    • Navigate to finndemy.com
    • Below the search bar, click “Become an Instructor”
  2. Complete Registration Form
    • First and last name
    • Username
    • Professional Email Address
    • Phone Number
    • Country/Region
  3. Create Strong Password
    • Minimum 8 characters
    • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Add numbers and special characters
    • Avoid common words or personal information
  4. Agree to the privacy policy and click “Submit”
  5. After successful registration you will receive a confirmation email
How to Log in
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • On the top right corner, click “Log In”
  • Enter your registered username and password
  • Tick the “Remember Me” box if using a trusted device for future auto-login
  • Click “Login”
  • You have now successfully logged in to your Finndemy account
Forgot Password? How to Reset
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click “Log In” in the top right area
  • Enter your email address and password and click Login
  • If your password is incorrect, click on “Lost your Password”
  • On the next page, enter the email you used to sign up and click “Reset Password”
  • You will see a confirmation that the password reset email has been sent. Check your email.
  • Check among your most recent emails.
  • Open the password reset email and click the “Click Here” link
  • You will be redirected to a page asking you to reset your password
  • Enter a new, strong password you will remember and click Save
  • Your browser may prompt you to save your password. If you're on a trusted device, you may click “Save”
  • You’ll see a confirmation that your password has been successfully reset. You can now log in with your updated credentials.
How to Log Out
  • Once you are logged in, go to the top right corner of the page.
  • Below the support email, your username will appear with a “Logout” icon.
  • Click “Log Out”. You will be signed out and redirected to the Finndemy homepage (finndemy.com).

Course Deletion & Restoration

How to Delete a Course

Method 1: From Course Editor

  • Open the course editor → Click “Move to Trash” in the top-left.

Method 2: From Course List

  • Go to Dashboard → Course List → Click trash icon under the course.

To Permanently Delete:

  • Go to the Trash tab → click trash icon again → confirm deletion.
  • Warning: This is irreversible.
How to Restore a Deleted Course
  • Go to Course List → Trash tab.
  • Click the Restore icon under the course you wish to recover.
  • The course will return to its previous state (draft or published).
